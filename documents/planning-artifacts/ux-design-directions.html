OLAF Health Dashboard

Design direction mockup — our committed visual system applied to all four categories.
Layout: 2.8" ILI9341 rectangle on the left in portrait orientation (240×320); two 1.28" GC9A01 round displays stacked on the right (each 240×240). Vertically symmetric about the faceplate midline.
Mockup shows circles rendered smaller than physical proportion would dictate — deliberately reduced to emphasise the small 1.28" form factor relative to the 2.8" rectangle. Content inside circles is proportionally scaled (~60% of spec values); firmware renders at the full 240×240 canvas at the committed token sizes (56 px headline numerals, 12 px labels, etc.).

Hardware: 2.8" ILI9341 rectangle on left in portrait (240×320 logical canvas); two 1.28" GC9A01 round displays on right, stacked (each 240×240 canvas). Vertically symmetric about the faceplate midline.
Typography: Inter (Regular 400, Medium 500, Bold 700), tabular numerals. Headline numeral 56 px Bold. Unit suffix 22 px Medium. Category label 12 px Medium caps. Timestamp 10 px Regular. Chart title 14 px Medium.
Status ring geometry: outer r = 118, inner r = 106, centre (120, 120), 12 px annulus — full 360° fill in the status colour.
Backgrounds: true OLED black #000000 everywhere.
